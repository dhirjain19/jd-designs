Master Bedroom
The overall design of the bedroom is modern, luxurious, and meticulously planned to provide comfort and convenience. The integration of the walk-in closet within the bedroom space is both practical and stylish, offering ample storage without compromising the room's aesthetics. The use of warm wood tones, plush textiles, and modern lighting fixtures creates a harmonious and serene environment ideal for rest and relaxation.
The ceiling has a recessed design with a modern fan and built-in LED strip lighting that creates a soft glow around the perimeter, enhancing the room's ambiance. Pendant lights hang on either side of the bed, adding a contemporary touch and additional lighting options.
The room features a warm and neutral color palette, with shades of beige, brown, and white. These colors create a calming and inviting atmosphere.
The centerpiece of the room is a large, plush bed with a soft, curved headboard. The bedding includes a mix of textures and neutral tones, adding to the cozy feel of the room.
The walls are adorned with a combination of wood paneling and sleek, white surfaces, creating a modern and elegant backdrop.

Small decorative items, such as a stylish handbag and magazines on the bench, add a personal touch to the space, making it feel lived-in and inviting.

A potted plant in the corner adds a touch of greenery, bringing life and freshness to the interior.
At the foot of the bed, there is a stylish upholstered bench with a gold metal frame, which serves as a functional and decorative piece. Nightstands on either side of the bed hold modern table lamps with sleek, cylindrical designs, providing soft lighting for the space.
The flooring is a combination of polished marble near the closet area and soft carpeting in the bedroom, providing both luxury and comfort. The use of black marble with white veining around the closet adds an element of sophistication and ties the space together.
