Coffee Shop
The design shows a modern and cozy interior. Large windows are visible in the background, allowing natural light to enter and offering a view of the cityscape outside. A ceiling fan is mounted above the coffee bar area, and an air conditioning unit is installed near the ceiling, ensuring the space is comfortable for patrons. The overall design is contemporary, with a blend of natural materials and minimalist decor, creating a welcoming environment for customers.
The lighting is soft and warm, with LED strip lights on the ceiling and along the walls, providing a comfortable and inviting ambiance.

There is a wooden table with a natural finish, surrounded by white chairs. The table is set with coffee cups and a decorative piece with flowers.

On the left wall, there is a minimalist wall clock with a circular design in black and gold tones. The wall has a textured finish, giving it a stylish look.
At the back, there is a coffee bar counter made of wood, with the words "COFFEE BAR" inscribed on it. There are two wooden stools at the counter, suggesting a spot for customers to sit and enjoy their drinks.
