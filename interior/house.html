House
The architectural design depicted in the image showcases a modern residential entrance featuring a blend of contemporary elements and materials. Overall, the architectural design is characterized by its modern, minimalist approach, with a focus on clean lines, contrasting colors, and the integration of natural elements. The entrance is designed to be both functional and aesthetically pleasing, creating a welcoming and elegant approach to the residence.
Main gate
The entrance is marked by a prominent gate structure. The gate itself features vertical bars with a combination of yellow and grey hues, creating a striking visual contrast. The gateposts are large, with one being a cylindrical concrete pillar and the other a rectangular frame, both in a light grey finish.
