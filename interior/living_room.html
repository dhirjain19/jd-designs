Living Room
The design shows a modern and minimalist living room interior. The room predominantly uses a white color palette with accents of green and grey. The walls and ceiling are white, creating a clean and bright atmosphere. The focal point of the room is a wall-mounted TV set within a large rectangular frame that appears to be made of marble or a marble-like material. The frame adds a touch of luxury to the space. Flanking the TV are vertical panels in green and grey, adding texture and depth to the design. These panels create a visually appealing backdrop for the TV. The overall design is modern and minimalist, focusing on clean lines, simple shapes, and a limited color palette to create a sophisticated and serene living environment.
Below the TV is a sleek, black shelving unit with closed storage. It offers a minimalist storage solution, keeping the space tidy and organized.

On the shelving unit, there are a few decorative items, including a small vase with branches and flowers, and a black cylindrical object, possibly a speaker or decorative container.

The ceiling features modern, rectangular recessed lights, providing ample illumination. The lighting fixtures are simple and complement the minimalist design of the room.
A part of a white chair with gold accents is visible on the left side of the image, suggesting a seating area. The chair adds a touch of elegance with its gold details.

The floor appears to be covered in white tiles or a similar material, contributing to the clean and modern aesthetic of the room.

A red vase with a plant is placed on the floor to the right of the TV unit. The plant adds a natural element and a pop of color to the otherwise monochromatic space.
